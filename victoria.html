Victoria Memorial

The Victoria Memorial, located in Kolkata, West Bengal, is an iconic symbol of colonial heritage and architectural grandeur.

Established in 1921, the Victoria Memorial features magnificent Indo-Saracenic Revival architecture.

Functioning as a memorial and museum, it stands as a tribute to Queen Victoria and the British Raj in India.

While not designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, it remains a major tourist destination in Kolkata.

The Victoria Memorial Hall is a notable structure within the memorial complex.

Its attractions include a museum, gardens, and historical exhibitions, drawing visitors from around the world.
